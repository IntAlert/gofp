What is
Peacebuilding?

Peacebuilding exists to prevent violence and create lasting peace. It focuses on the long term changes needed for peace by looking at the root causes of conflict.

More Info ▸

For success it needs to be inclusive from the grassroots to policy level. Everyone has a part to play in building peace. Small everyday actions can help us to break down barriers and understand each other. To learn more please visit:
